Backup & Recovery

Fast Close

Backup & Recovery is a low-friction, high-close-rate add-on that resolves a genuine operational risk. Ask admins and ops leaders about their current data recovery plan — most don't have one. Frame as insurance rather than software, price it low relative to downtime risk, and it rarely goes through extended legal review.

Discovery question: "If someone deleted 10,000 contact records tomorrow morning, what would you do?" The silence that follows usually closes the deal. Follow with a same-day quote.

Your customers' contracts are coming up for renewal in the next 6 months. Get ahead of it now — having a buying conversation early avoids the January PI crunch and positions you as a proactive partner rather than someone who just shows up with a bill.

Q3 Early Renewals

Customers with renewal dates falling in Q3 are best contacted now — early renewal conversations keep you in control of timing and avoid the discount-pressure environment that builds when customers feel the clock ticking. Position as giving them time to plan, not time to shop around.

Tactic: Lead with value conversation first (what's landed, what's next), then introduce the renewal timeline. Customers who feel served renew early. Customers who feel sold to wait until the last minute.

Fast-Cycle Buyers — Last 2 FYs

Customers who have purchased additional products or expanded their contract in each of the last two fiscal years are your most reliable buyers. They buy on a rhythm — and that rhythm is now. Identify the pattern, understand what triggered each purchase, and make the next conversation about what logically comes next in their journey.

Why they buy again: They get value, they grow, and they already trust the vendor. The sales motion here is shorter than new business — skip qualification, go straight to discovery about their current state and what's next on their roadmap.
